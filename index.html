Cookie Monster Is Hungry!!

Can you guess what kind of cookie Cookie Monster want to eat?


Guess what kind of cookie Cookie Monster wants to eat before he gets hangry! You will have 3 guesses. Press the corresponding letter to the cookie you want to give him. Guess correctly and he munches! Guess incorrectly and prepare fo the hangry! Choose a cookie to begin!

  • Chocolate Chip = c
  • Oatmeal = o
  • Snickerdoodle = s
  • Monster Cookie = m
  • Peanut Butter = p
  • Gingerbread = g
  • Fortune Cookie = f
  • White Chocolate Chip = w
